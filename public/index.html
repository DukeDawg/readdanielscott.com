About Daniel Scott

Thrillers where the case is only the visible wound.

Daniel Scott is the dark-crime and supernatural-thriller identity for readers who want stories that move fast, hit hard, and leave a bruise. The books range from FBI serial-killer investigations to psychological descent, escaped-monster manhunts, and haunted-family suspense.

The connective tissue is simple: dangerous people, damaged heroes, old sins, and the suspicion that solving the case may not be the same thing as surviving it.

Quick answers

Reader FAQ

Which Daniel Scott book should I read first?

For most crime-thriller readers, start with The Surgeon’s Scalpel, book one of Heinous Crimes Unit. For supernatural suspense, start with A Friend of the Dead.

Do I need to read the series in order?

Yes inside each series. The four major series lanes are separate enough that you can choose whichever flavor you want first.

Where can I buy the books?

The current primary retailer path is Amazon. Every book and series lane on this page links to Amazon directly.

Is there a boxed set?

Yes: Heinous Crimes Unit Boxed Set Books 1–8 is surfaced near the top because binge readers are the best kind of trouble.
